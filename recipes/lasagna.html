Lasagna

Nutrition

Calories: 427kcal | Carbohydrates: 39g | Protein: 31g | Fat: 17g | Saturated Fat: 9g

Ingredients

  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • 1 (24 to 26-ounce) jar marinara sauce
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 (12-ounce) package no-boil, oven ready lasagna noodles
  • 1 (15-ounce) container ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 425°F and lightly spray an 11×7-inch baking dish with nonstick cooking spray.
  2. Brown the ground beef over medium-high heat and drain the excess grease away. Return the meat to the skillet over medium-low heat and add the marinara and 1 cup water. Stir to combine and heat through.
  3. Layer about 1/4 of the meat sauce evenly in the bottom of the prepared pan. Cover with a layer of the uncooked pasta. Add about half of the ricotta cheese and spread evenly. Sprinkle about 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese. Top with and 1/4 of the sauce, then more pasta, the remaining ricotta, and 1/3 more of the mozzarella cheese. Add another 1/4 of the sauce, then another layer of pasta. Top with the remaining sauce and sprinkle with the remaining mozzarella cheese.
  4. Wrap the dish tightly with aluminum foil and bake for a hour or until the pasta is tender. Allow to rest for about 10 minutes before serving.
